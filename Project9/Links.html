Rent-A-Pet: Favorite Links

Pet-Co

Very good store to find resources like food, leashes, etc. All sorts of products available for your pet's needs during their stay with you! Can also ask for advice about how to take care of your pet and what's best for them.

Animal Foundation

A good website for how to take care of your pets. If you've never had any pets before, this is a good way to learn the basics. We want both you and your pet to have a great time together and this website can help with that!

Wiki How

Another good website for how to take care of your pets. This teaches you the basics and gives pictures as well. Good for people who never have had any pets.

ASPCA

If you have a dog staying with you, this a great website to look at! Gives gerneral tips to take care of them. Gives a good list of what you should have when your pet stays with you.

Kirsty Lou's Doggie Doo's

If you have a dog staying with you, this a great store if your dog needs a trim! This place is also good if you want to learn how to take care of your dogs hair. This place takes appointments so I juggest to schedule sooner rather than later!
